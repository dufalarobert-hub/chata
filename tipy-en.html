Travel Tips

10 travel tips from Cottage Bardejov

Real distances and times so you can quickly plan your day. Just pick a card and go.

UNESCO Center of Bardejov

5 min by car / 20 min walk from cottage

Take a walk through the most beautiful square in Slovakia. Visit the Basilica of St. Egidius and the town hall.

Bardejov Spa & Colonnade

10 min by car

Relax in historic spas where Empress Sisi was treated. Taste the healing water directly at the colonnade.

Zborov Castle (Makovica)

12 min by car + 35 min hike

Easy hiking to one of the largest ruins in Slovakia. Fire pits and beautiful views are the reward for the climb.

Open-Air Museum - Museum of Folk Architecture

10 min by car (in Spa)

The oldest open-air museum in Slovakia. You'll see beautiful wooden cottages and rare small churches.

Skiing in Regetovka

18–20 min by car

Popular local resort Regetovka with family atmosphere and good skiing conditions.

Wooden Church Hervartov (UNESCO)

15 min by car

The oldest and best-preserved wooden church in Slovakia (a true world rarity).

Natural Swimming Pool Makovica

18 min by car

Refreshing water in a natural setting under the forest in summer.

Wellness at Hotel Ozón

10 min by car

Modern wellness complex with pools and sauna world available to the public.

Sunny Farm - Stebnícka Huta

20 min by car

Agritourism, horseback riding and great hunting restaurant near the border.

Cycling Trails and Singletracks

Directly from cottage or 15 min by car

Healing singletracks in Bardejov Spa or cycling trail to Mníchovský potok.
