About the Cottage

Amenities and layout for your comfort

Quiet cottage area just 20 minutes pleasant walk from the historic Town Hall Square in Bardejov. Capacity ideally 6 people (comfort), maximum 8 people.

Ground Floor

Large living room with fireplace (sleeping option for 2 people), fully equipped kitchen and bathroom with WC and spacious shower corner.

Upper Floor

Two separate bedrooms, each with capacity for 3 beds (layout 3+3). Comfortable mattresses and plenty of storage space.

Exterior

Fenced property (safety for children and dogs), large covered terrace and outdoor covered grill for year-round grilling.

Location and activities

What to do nearby

Choose from tips for winter sports, summer refreshment and unique attractions. Everything within easy reach of the cottage.

Winter sports

  • • Ski Regetovka (18 km) – popular local resort.
  • • Tylicz Ski & Master Ski (Poland, 18 km) – top family resorts.
  • • Jaworzyna Krynicka (Poland, 30 km) – longest cable car in the area.

Summer and water

  • • Natural swimming pool Makovica (14 km towards Svidník).
  • • Bardejov Spa (5 km) – wellness at Hotel Ozón, open-air museum, swimming pool.
  • • Aquaruthenia Water World Svidník (20 km).

Unique attractions

  • • UNESCO center of Bardejov (Basilica of St. Egidius).
  • • Tree canopy walkway (Wieża Widokowa) in Krynica.
  • • Hiking to Zborov Castle or Kamenná hora lookout tower.
