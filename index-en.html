About the Cottage

Amenities and layout for your comfort

Quiet cottage area just 20 minutes pleasant walk from the historic Town Hall Square in Bardejov. Capacity ideally 6 people (comfort), maximum 8 people.

Ground Floor

Large living room with fireplace (sleeping option for 2 people), fully equipped kitchen and bathroom with WC and spacious shower corner.

Upper Floor

Two separate bedrooms, each with capacity for 3 beds (layout 3+3). Comfortable mattresses and plenty of storage space.

Exterior

Fenced property (safety for children and dogs), large covered terrace and outdoor covered grill for year-round grilling.
